## Verse

**Verse 4**

And the voice spoke plainly:

Young one,
take care of thy business
while the currency is still in thy hand.

Do not wait
until the door is locked,
the phone is timed,
and thy freedom depends
on people who were never built
to carry the weight of thy choices.

Buy not the shine
before thou hast bought protection.

Lift not the chain
above the lawyer.

Place not the picture
above the plan.

For what profit is there
in looking rich
while standing unprepared
for the day trouble calls thy name?

If thou hast money for attention,
then thou hast money for order.

If thou hast money for the lens,
then thou hast money for the file.

If thou hast money for the wheel,
then thou hast money for the paper
that keeps thy mother
from losing sleep over thy name.

Let not thy family
become collateral
for thy lack of preparation.

Let not the house,
the car,
the jewelry,
the prayers of thy people,
be dragged into the fire
because thou wanted applause
more than structure.

The street will praise thy image
and still punish thy ignorance.

The crowd will love thy outfit
and vanish when the bond is due.

The hearts will rise on the screen,
but they will not sign the paper.

The comments will call thee blessed,
but they will not stand in the courtroom.

So take care of thy business.

While thou art free,
prepare for pressure.

While thou art paid,
prepare for consequence.

While thou art moving,
prepare for the day
movement gets restricted.

This is not fear.

This is order.

This is not weakness.

This is wisdom.

Because the same life
that lets thee pop it
will one day ask:

Who did you become
when nobody was clapping?

And what did you put in place
before the storm came?

This is the fourth principle:

Do not make thy family pay
for the business
thou refused to handle
while thou had the chance.

## Reader Notes

### Core Message

This verse is about preparation before crisis. Looking successful, being liked, and having visible money means nothing if the basic business of protection, legal readiness, family responsibility, and personal order has been ignored.

### Pattern Being Exposed

The pattern is performance before preparation. People spend money to be seen, praised, and validated, but avoid handling the serious infrastructure that protects them when pressure comes. The loop is broken by putting order before image.

### Raw Theme

Young people should not spend all their money on appearance, jewelry, cars, clothes, and online attention while ignoring the real responsibilities that protect them and their families during legal or life emergencies.

### Principles

- Do not make family collateral for poor preparation.
- Image does not replace legal, financial, or personal structure.
- Visibility can bring praise, but order brings protection.
- Buy protection before performance.
- Handle the foundation before the crisis arrives.
