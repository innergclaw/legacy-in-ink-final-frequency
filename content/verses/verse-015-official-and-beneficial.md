## Verse

**Verse 15**

And the teacher said:
Though our mentalities
may be different,
I can still learn
from the man
who carries fruit.

For difference
does not disqualify
wisdom.

But emptiness
must be examined.

Every person
in thy circle
must be
official
and beneficial.

Not perfect.

Not identical.

Not always
in agreement.

But official
in character,
and beneficial
to the becoming
of thy life.

Tell the young people:
If thou speakest
to someone,
and all they carry
is negativity,
watch closely.

If they never bring
inspiration,
watch closely.

If they never bring
uncommon information,
watch closely.

If every conversation
leaves thee smaller,
heavier,
angrier,
or more confused,
watch closely.

For some friendships
do not fight thee
with weapons.

They simply keep thee
in a battleship
with success.

They keep thee
arguing with thy future
while calling it loyalty.

They keep thee
close to their limits
until thy own vision
starts speaking
with their mouth.

Love is good.

History is real.

Affection has its place.

But every voice
around thee
should not have
equal access
to the direction
of thy life.

When people speak,
listen for fruit.

Listen for something
that compels thee
to become better.

Listen for information
that stretches thee.

Listen for correction
that sharpens thee.

Listen for language
that calls the higher man
out of hiding.

And if a person
cannot compel thee
toward the best thee,
then distance
becomes instruction.

For not everyone
who likes thee
is assigned
to build thee.

Not everyone
who laughs with thee
is qualified
to guide thee.

Not everyone
who knows thy past
deserves a seat
beside thy future.

Let the circle
be official.

Let the circle
be beneficial.

Let every voice
near thee
carry something
that helps thee rise.

---

## Reader Notes

### Core Message

Your circle should add value, correction, inspiration, information, or accountability. Love alone is not enough reason to keep every voice close.

### Pattern Being Exposed

The pattern is keeping relationships out of history, guilt, comfort, or entertainment while those same relationships quietly oppose growth. The loop breaks by auditing the fruit of every connection and requiring the circle to support becoming.

---

## Archive Notes

### Principles

- Difference can still teach; emptiness cannot.
- Every close voice should be official and beneficial.
- Negativity without inspiration becomes spiritual drag.
- Love does not require unlimited access.
- A future needs a circle that can speak to it.

### Legacy In Ink Frame

This verse belongs to the youth-circle audit. It turns friendship into a standard of stewardship and asks the reader to measure access by fruit.
